# Unit 1 Question Bank

## Definition Questions

1. What is globalisation?
2. What is global media?
3. What is local content?
4. What is representation?
5. What is cultural identity?
6. What is media regulation?
7. What is platform power?
8. What is Western media?

## Relationship Questions

1. Explain the relationship between globalisation and cultural identity.
2. Explain the relationship between streaming platforms and local content.
3. Explain the relationship between representation and identity.
4. Explain the relationship between media regulation and cultural production.
5. Explain the relationship between platform power and local media industries.

## Compare Questions

1. Compare global media and local media.
2. Compare cultural identity and national identity.
3. Compare local content and global content.
4. Compare platform power and government regulation.
5. Compare Western media and global media.

## Discuss Questions

1. Discuss whether globalisation supports or threatens local culture.
2. Discuss why local content remains important in global media.
3. Discuss whether national identity still matters in global media.
4. Discuss how streaming platforms affect national media industries.

## Evaluate Questions

1. Evaluate whether global streaming platforms help or harm local media industries.
2. Evaluate the usefulness of "Western media" as a category.
3. Evaluate whether media regulation is necessary in a global platform economy.
