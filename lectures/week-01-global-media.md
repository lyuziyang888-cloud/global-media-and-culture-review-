# Week 1 Lecture: Global Media

## Lecture Focus

Week 1 introduces the course's central question: what is global media?

## Teacher Emphasis

Based on the lecture direction, the important questions are:

- What makes media global?
- What makes media local?
- How do we define national media?
- Does nation still matter in global media?
- What is Western media?
- Why should we question Western-centric thinking?
