# Media Imperialism

## 30-Second Definition

Media imperialism describes unequal international media relationships in which powerful countries and corporations dominate ownership, production, distribution and cultural influence.

## Keywords To Memorise

- unequal flows
- ownership
- domination
- core and periphery
- cultural influence
- political economy
- US power

## Key Features

- Media power is concentrated in a small number of countries and corporations.
- Economic and technological dominance can support cultural influence.
- International media flows are structured by unequal resources and ownership.
- New technologies can renew rather than eliminate older power relations.

## Why It Matters

The concept provides the historical framework for understanding why global communication does not automatically produce balanced cultural exchange.
