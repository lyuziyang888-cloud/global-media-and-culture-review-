# Cultural Identity

## 30-Second Definition

Cultural identity refers to the shared meanings, values, symbols, stories and practices through which a group understands itself.

## Keywords To Memorise

- shared meaning
- values
- symbols
- belonging
- culture
- representation
- identity

## Key Features

- Built through stories, images, media and cultural practices.
- Can be national, regional, local, Indigenous or transnational.
- Can be strengthened when people see themselves represented.
- Can be challenged by dominant global media.

## Why It Matters

Cultural identity matters because media helps people understand who they are, where they belong and how their culture is seen by others.

## Relationships

- [Representation](representation.md) shapes cultural identity.
- [Globalisation](globalisation.md) can spread or weaken cultural identity.
- [Local Content](local-content.md) can protect cultural identity.
- [Media Regulation](media-regulation.md) can support cultural identity through funding and content rules.

## Example

Canadian screen productions can express cultural identity through Canadian locations, creators, actors, public funding and national symbols.
