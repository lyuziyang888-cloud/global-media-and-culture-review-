# Week 1 Tutorial

## Tutorial Focus

The tutorial turns definitions into analysis questions.

## Key Discussion Questions

- What makes a media text Canadian?
- What is local content?
- Would `Heated Rivalry` count as Western media?
- Who gets counted as "the West" and who does not?
- Why does classification matter?

## Exam Conversion

Tutorial question:

```text
What makes it Canadian?
```

Exam version:

```text
Discuss how national identity can be constructed through media production, representation and distribution.
```

Tutorial question:

```text
Would this count as Western media?
```

Exam version:

```text
Evaluate the usefulness and limits of the category "Western media" in global media studies.
```
