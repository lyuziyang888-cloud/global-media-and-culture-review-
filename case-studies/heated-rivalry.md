# Heated Rivalry

## Case Type

Local content / national identity / Canadian screen production

## What This Case Shows

`Heated Rivalry` is useful because it raises the question of what makes a media text Canadian.

## Useful For These Concepts

- local content
- national identity
- cultural identity
- media industry
- global distribution
- global vs local

## Analysis Points

- Is nationality based on cast?
- Is nationality based on crew?
- Is nationality based on creator?
- Is nationality based on filming location?
- Is nationality based on story and symbols?
- Is nationality based on funding and industry?

## Exam Use

Use this case when answering questions about how local content is defined or how national identity is represented through media production.

## Short Example Sentence

`Heated Rivalry` shows that local content is not defined by one factor alone, because production, creators, setting, funding and cultural symbols can all contribute to national identity.
