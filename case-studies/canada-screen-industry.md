# Canada Screen Industry

## Case Type

National media industry / globalisation / local content / regulation

## What This Case Shows

Canada's screen industry shows the tension between global market reach and the protection of local cultural identity.

## Exam Use

Use this case when a question asks about:

- globalisation and local culture
- why governments regulate media platforms
- how national identity is represented in media
- whether global platforms support or threaten local media industries

## Short Example Sentence

Canada's screen industry demonstrates that global media can increase the international visibility of local content, while also creating concerns about cultural dependence on larger US-based platforms and industries.
