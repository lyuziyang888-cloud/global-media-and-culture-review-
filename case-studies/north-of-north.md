# North of North

## Case Type

Indigenous representation / local content / global distribution

## What This Case Shows

`North of North` is useful because it links Indigenous-led storytelling with global streaming distribution.

## Useful For These Concepts

- representation
- Indigenous representation
- cultural identity
- local content
- streaming platforms
- globalisation

## Analysis Points

- Indigenous creators and communities can shape their own representation.
- Local stories can reach global audiences through platforms such as Netflix.
- Global distribution can increase visibility but also depends on platform power.

## Exam Use

Use this case when a question asks about representation, authorship, identity or whether global platforms can support local cultures.

## Short Example Sentence

`North of North` shows that global platforms can increase the visibility of local and Indigenous representation when communities have meaningful control over storytelling.
