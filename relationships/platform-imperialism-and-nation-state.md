# Platform Imperialism and the Nation-State

## Relationship In One Sentence

Platform imperialism is strengthened by a reciprocal relationship in which platforms lobby states for favourable expansion while states use platform infrastructure for economic, political and security objectives.

## How They Interact

```text
Platform revenue and lobbying
-> favourable policy and market access
-> global platform expansion
-> data and infrastructure useful to the state
-> stronger corporate-state power
```

## Case Study

Jin links Facebook's US lobbying, the US free-flow-of-information doctrine, Cambridge Analytica and state surveillance programs to the continuing importance of nation-state power.

## Evaluation

Digital platforms operate globally, but this does not make the nation-state irrelevant. Jin's argument is that state and corporate power become intertwined rather than one replacing the other.

## Possible Exam Questions

- Explain the relationship between platform corporations and nation-states.
- Does platform globalisation weaken the nation-state?
- Why is platform imperialism political as well as economic?
