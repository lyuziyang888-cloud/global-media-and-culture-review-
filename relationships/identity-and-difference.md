# Identity and Difference

## Relationship In One Sentence

Identity is constructed through similarity with those understood as "us" and difference from those classified as "them."

## How They Interact

```text
Symbols and categories
-> similarity and belonging
-> difference and boundaries
-> identity positions
-> possible inclusion or exclusion
```

Difference is relational rather than self-contained. It can support belonging, but unequal classifications can turn difference into othering, stereotyping and exclusion.

## Case Study

The Jackie Kay example in Woodward shows how Black Scottish identity is challenged by an unequal assumption that a "real" Scottish identity is white.

## Exam Answer Skeleton

Define identity and difference, explain how group boundaries create belonging, show how power assigns unequal value to differences, apply the case, and evaluate whether difference can also be reclaimed as a positive collective identity.

## Source Anchor

Woodward, "Questions of Identity," pp. 6-8 and 31-34.
