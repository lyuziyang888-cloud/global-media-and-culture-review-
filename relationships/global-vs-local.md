# Global vs Local

## Relationship In One Sentence

The global and the local are not opposites; global media often depends on local content, and local content can circulate globally.

## Global

Global refers to media flows, platforms, audiences or industries that operate across national borders.

## Local

Local refers to media connected to a specific place, community, culture or nation.

## Key Tension

```text
Local identity and stories
↓
Need funding and distribution
↓
Global platforms
↓
Wider reach but less local control
```

## Case Study

`Heated Rivalry` can be analysed through the question of what makes media Canadian: production, cast, crew, setting, story, funding, ownership or audience.

## Possible Exam Questions

- What is the difference between global and local media?
- Can media be both local and global?
- Why is the local still important in global media?
