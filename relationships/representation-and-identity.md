# Representation and Identity

## Relationship In One Sentence

Representation shapes identity because media portrayals influence how people, communities and nations understand themselves and are understood by others.

## A: Representation

Representation is how people, places and cultures are portrayed in media.

## B: Identity

Identity is how individuals or groups understand who they are and where they belong.

## How They Interact

```text
Media representation
↓
Visibility and meaning
↓
Audience understanding
↓
Cultural or national identity
```

## Case Study

`North of North` is useful for Indigenous representation because it foregrounds Indigenous creators, performers, locations and stories.

## Exam Answer Skeleton

Representation and identity are closely connected. Media representation gives visibility and meaning to particular groups, places and cultures. When communities control their own stories, representation can support stronger and more authentic identity. However, when representation is controlled by outsiders, it can simplify or stereotype identity.
