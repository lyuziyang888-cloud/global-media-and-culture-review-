# Streaming Platforms and Local Content

## Relationship In One Sentence

Streaming platforms can help local content reach global audiences, but they also create dependence on large companies that control distribution and visibility.

## A: Streaming Platforms

Streaming platforms distribute content online and often operate internationally.

## B: Local Content

Local content represents specific places, communities, nations or cultures.

## How They Interact

```text
Local production
↓
Streaming platform distribution
↓
Global audience access
↓
Greater cultural visibility
```

But:

```text
Streaming platform power
↓
Control over funding, visibility and promotion
↓
Local media industry dependence
```

## Case Study

Netflix can distribute `North of North` internationally, while debates in Canada show concern over whether platforms should support local and Indigenous content financially.

## Possible Exam Questions

- How do streaming platforms affect local content?
- Do streaming platforms support or threaten local culture?
- Explain the relationship between platform power and local media.
